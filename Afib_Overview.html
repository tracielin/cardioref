Should my patient with Atrial Fibrillation participate in organized sports?

Atrial Fibrillation Atrial Flutter EAT, AVNRT, AVRT, & WPW PVCs Non-sustained ventricular tachycardia (NSVT) Monomorphic VT, sustained Polymorphic VT or VF, sustained

Atrial Fibrillation

Should have workup including:

  • ECG
  • Echocardiogram
  • TFTs
  • Query for drug use

"Athletes with AF should undergo a workup that includes thyroid function tests, queries for drug use, ECG, and echocardiogram (Class I; Level of Evidence B)."

Low risk AF, well tolerated, self-terminating

"Athletes with low-risk AF that is well tolerated and self-terminating may participate in all competitive sports without therapy (Class I; Level of Evidence C)."

Can participate in all sports without therapy

On antithrombotic therapy other than ASA

"In athletes with AF, when antithrombotic therapy, other than aspirin, is indicated, it is reasonable to consider the bleeding risk in the context of the specific sport before clearance (Class IIa; Level of Evidence C)."

Consider bleeding risk before clearance

Ablation candidate

"Catheter ablation for AF could obviate the need for rate control or antiarrhythmic drugs and should be considered (Class IIa; Level of Evidence B)."

Consider ablation for AF
