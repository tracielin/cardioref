"The treatment of choice for athletes with regular, acute-onset, symptomatic SVTs should be catheter ablation (Class I; Level of Evidence C)."

Consider ablation

Short refractory period bypass tracts, h/o AF

"Athletes with short refractory period bypass tracts capable of anterograde conduction and a history of paroxysmal AF should have an ablation of the accessory pathway before clearance for competitive sports because of risk for life-threatening arrhythmias (Class I; Level of Evidence B)."

Should have ablation before clearance for sports
