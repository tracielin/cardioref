Asymptomatic, no structural heart disease

"Asymptomatic athletes with no structural heart disease and first-degree AV block (PR interval < 0.3 ms) can participate in all competitive sports unless there are findings that indicate that the person is at risk for progression to higher-degree block that would symptoms (Class I; Level of Evidence C)."

All sports = acceptable unless findings indicating risk for progression to higher-degree block

Asymptomatic, type 1 second-degree AV block with exercise

"Asymptomatic athletes with first-degree AV block, in whom type I second-degree AV block appears with exercise, should be evaluated further for possible intra-His or infra-His block with EPS (Class I; Level of Evidence C)."

Should undergo EP study

Structural heart disease

"If structural heart disease is present, athletic restrictions should be recommended as appropriate for the type of structural heart disease (Class I; Level of Evidence C)."

Depends on structural heart disease
